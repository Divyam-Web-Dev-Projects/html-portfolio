The Best Movies According To Divyam

My Top 3 movies of all time.

RRR

This is my favourite movie of all time. It's background is on British period when they were ruling over India and suppressing the innocent people. There are various emotions embedded in this movie such as patriotism, friendship, responsibility towards family. It includes various action sequences which are worthy to watch over 100 times.

Vikram

This is thriller and action based movie. It is about a secret national agency agents.

KGF

This movie is also one of my most watched movie. This movie is about a gangster who gets control over the gold mine and become the most feared and dangerous man of the world. The movie also showcase the love and sacrifice of a mother towards his son and a promise, the mother gets from his son " To become the richest person of entire world"
